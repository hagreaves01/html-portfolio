My top three movies of All time

Naruto

Naruto is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Masashi Kishimoto. It tells the story of Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja who seeks recognition from his peers and dreams of becoming the Hokage, the leader of his village. The series is set in a fictional world where ninjas use supernatural abilities to fight and protect their villages. It explores themes of friendship, perseverance, and the struggle for acceptance.

One Piece

One Piece is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Eiichiro Oda. It follows the adventures of Monkey D. Luffy, a young pirate with the ability to stretch his body like rubber after eating a Devil Fruit. Luffy and his crew, the Straw Hat Pirates, search for the ultimate treasure known as "One Piece" to become the Pirate King. The series is known for its rich world-building, diverse characters, and themes of freedom and adventure.

Bleach

Bleach is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Tite Kubo. It follows the story of Ichigo Kurosaki, a teenager who gains the abilities of a Soul Reaper after accidentally obtaining them from Rukia Kuchiki, a Soul Reaper herself. Ichigo's new powers compel him to protect the living world from evil spirits known as Hollows and to guide the souls of the deceased to the afterlife. The series combines action, supernatural elements, and themes of duty and sacrifice.
